Air traffic in Australia

Australia is a country who has developed aviation industry. International and domestic aviation connects Australians with each other and with the rest of the world. It is also central to the Australian economy.
The Australian Government, through the department, contributes to the prosperity of the economy and the wellbeing of all Australians by fostering a viable, competitive and safe aviation industry.
This visualisation will mainly focus on domestic aviation about Australia in recent years. It will analysis the quantitative metrics about avaition and it will use graph to answer several questions about domestic aviation.

HOW DOES THE AIR TRAFFIC CHANGES
IN RECENT YEARS?

From 1984 to 2019, the total passenger, aircrafts, distance and seats grows. However, the total distance grows slower than other values, we can inference that the airlines in Australia use planes which can take more passengers.
However, these four indicators except for total distance drop sharply in 2020 due to COVID-19, they reached the number before 2000. The total distance didn't not be influenced, it may because the cargo aviation was still working and took the position of passenger aviation.

WHAT IS THE MOST POPULAR ROUTE
IN AUSTRALIA DOMESTIC LINES?

The most popular domestic line in Australia in Melbourne-Sydney, which had the largest passengers count from 2018 to 2020. The top-10 lines in Australia are also fixed in recent years, but Brisbane to Cairns and Townsville lines became one of the top-10 in 2020.
